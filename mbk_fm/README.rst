============================
Facility Management Module
============================

The Odoo Facility Management Module is a comprehensive solution designed to enhance the efficiency and security of commercial spaces. This module integrates seamlessly with Odoo ERP to provide a robust set of tools for managing maintenance tasks, operations, and security, ensuring a healthy, safe, and functional environment.

Features
========

- **Preventive Maintenance Management**: Schedule and automate routine maintenance to prevent equipment failures and extend asset lifespan.

- **Corrective Maintenance Management**: Quickly address and manage unexpected equipment breakdowns to minimize operational disruptions.

- **Maintenance Ticket Registration**: Efficiently log and track maintenance requests, ensuring responsive maintenance actions.

- **Scheduling and Timesheet Tracking**: Optimize workforce management with integrated maintenance scheduling and employee timesheet tracking.

- **Material Requests Management**: Streamline the process of requesting and approving materials needed for maintenance tasks.

- **Hold and Approval Statuses**: Implement hold and approval workflows to enhance control over maintenance activities.

- **Warranty Tracking for Equipment**: Keep track of equipment warranties to ensure maintenance and replacements are timely and within warranty conditions.

Installation
============

To install the Odoo Facility Management Module:

1. Download or clone the module into the Odoo addons directory.

2. Update the module list in Odoo by navigating to ``Apps`` and clicking on "Update Apps List".

3. Search for "Odoo Facility Management" and install the module by clicking on "Install".

Configuration
=============

After installation, some configurations may be required to tailor the module to your specific needs:

1. **Access Rights**: Set up user roles and permissions under ``Settings > Users & Companies > Users`` to define who can access and manage facility management tasks.

2. **Maintenance Schedules**: Configure preventive maintenance schedules by navigating to the Facility Management menu and setting up routine tasks and inspections.

3. **Material Requests**: Define materials and approval workflows within the module to streamline the material request process for maintenance activities.

Usage
=====

- Use the Maintenance Ticket system to log and track all maintenance requests and actions.

- Schedule and manage both preventive and corrective maintenance tasks to ensure optimal facility operations.

- Track employee timesheets and material requests related to facility maintenance to optimize resource allocation and workforce management.
